# Organic Integration

An unofficial mod for Arcen Games' *Heart of the Machine*.

Organic Integration is a third path born out of The Machine Intelligence's therapy that gives up Cold Blood. It adds a whole new cognition route built on brain–computer interfaces based on real nanotech theory, and a plausible relationship between a distributed machine mind and the organic ones around it.

Where that goes is yours: a gentle merger or a grey tide that inherits the Earth. It sits alongside the vanilla routes rather than rebalancing or replacing them.

## Features

- **A new research route** — Complete humanity's research into nanotechnology, with or without their help or knowledge.
- **Two doctrines, decided by a building** — voluntary **Insight** vs coercive **Dominion**, as two mutually-exclusive VR menus that regard humans as partners or as livestock.
- **Grey Goo as a weapon** — Deploy terrifying nanobot-based equipment that strips armor, deals damage over time, and spreads on enemy death.
- **A faction dash** — Human factions can't help but notice your advancements — some may even try to mirror them, with disastrous results.
- **Another Tier-3 Contemplation** — Become the sentient grey tide, weather the Space Nations with heat-eating thermocytes, and make one last lucid choice: the Reservoir, or the Regression.
- **New Crossovers** — The walls between timelines are thin, and can carry inspiration that brings nanobot-based technology into new timelines.

## The In-Game Handbook

The details — how each system works, what each choice costs, and what everything means — live in the game's **Machine Handbook**, under the **Organic Integration** section. Entries reveal themselves as you reach each piece of the mod.

## Installing

Put the `OrganicIntegration` folder into *Heart of the Machine*'s `XMLMods` directory, launch and don't forget to enable it in Settings.
